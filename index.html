Hello! My name is Nureddin, and I am a PhD (DPhil) student at the University of Oxford. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Amro Awad and I am a member of the OSCAR lab.

kamadan@kamadan-xps:~$ cat research.txt

My main research interests lie on the intersection of computer architecture, operating systems and security. Broadly, I am passionate about building secure and reliable systems and I believe research in this direction has a major potential positive impact on the society. My current focus is on hardware security, with recent efforts specifically centered on the Rowhammer vulnerability.
